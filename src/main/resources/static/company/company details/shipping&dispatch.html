Shipping & Dispatch Policy

Know how your order is processed and delivered

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation.

Your order will be packed carefully and dispatched from our warehouse. Once dispatched, you will receive tracking details via email.

  • Local Delivery: 2–3 days
  • Domestic Shipping: 3–7 days
  • Remote Areas: 5–10 days
  • Free shipping for orders above ₹1000
  • Standard delivery: ₹50
  • Express delivery: ₹120

After dispatch, a tracking ID will be sent to your email or SMS. You can track your order on the courier partner website.

If your order is delayed or damaged, please contact our support team immediately.
