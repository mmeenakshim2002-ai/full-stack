Privacy Policy

At MRM Handcraft, we value the trust our patrons place in us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or make a purchase.

Information We Collect

To fulfill your order and provide the best experience, we collect the following:

  • Identity Information: Your name and contact details.
  • Delivery Information: Shipping and billing addresses.
  • Payment Data: Processed securely through our payment partners (we do not store your full credit card numbers).
  • Technical Data: Your IP address and browsing behavior to help us improve our website's performance.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly for the following purposes:

  • To process and ship your handcrafted items.
  • To send order confirmations and tracking updates.
  • To respond to your inquiries or custom order requests.
  • With your permission, to send occasional updates about new collections or artisan stories.

Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. We only share data with trusted partners necessary to run our business, such as:

  • Courier Services: To deliver your items to your doorstep.
  • Payment Processors: To ensure your transactions are safe and encrypted.
  • Legal Requirements: Only if required by law to protect our rights or comply with a judicial proceeding.

Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your data is contained behind secured networks and is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights to such systems.

Cookies

Our website uses "cookies" to enhance your experience. Cookies allow us to remember the items in your shopping cart and understand your preferences for future visits. You can choose to turn off cookies through your browser settings.

Your Rights

As an MRM Handcraft patron, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request that we correct or delete your information.
  • Opt-out of any marketing emails at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link.
