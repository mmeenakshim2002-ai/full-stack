Return & Refund Policy

Learn about our return process and refund guidelines

Products can be returned within 7 days of delivery if they meet the following conditions:

  • Product is unused and in original packaging
  • Invoice or receipt is available
  • Product is not damaged by the customer
  • Personal care items
  • Opened or used products
  • Digital downloads

Once the returned product is received and inspected, we will notify you about the approval of your refund.

  • Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
  • Refund will be credited to your original payment method.

If you receive a defective or damaged product, you may request a replacement within 48 hours of delivery.

Customers may need to pay return shipping charges unless the product is defective or incorrect.
