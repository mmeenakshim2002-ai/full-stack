About us

Welcome to MRM Handmade Craft !

Welcome to Handmade Craft, your trusted home for beautiful handmade creations. Established in 2002, our journey began with a simple passion for art and a love for creating meaningful handmade products.

For more than 20 years, we have been crafting wall hanging products, quilling paper wall art, and woollen bags with care, creativity, and dedication. Every piece we make reflects the warmth of tradition and the charm of true handmade craftsmanship.

At Handmade Craft, we believe that handmade art carries emotion, patience, and the unique touch of the artist’s hands. Over the years, our small creative hobby has grown into a brand that people trust for quality, originality, and artistic beauty.

We continue to use high-quality materials and a blend of traditional techniques with modern designs to create products that bring color, personality, and joy into your life. Whether it’s a decorative wall hanging or a stylish woollen bag, each product is made with love and purpose.

Thank you for supporting our journey since 2002. Your appreciation inspires us to keep crafting beautiful things every day.

Our mission

To create unique, long-lasting handmade products that bring beauty, creativity, and happiness into every home.

Our vision

To remain a trusted handmade brand that preserves traditional art, supports creativity, and delivers eco-friendly handcrafted products.
