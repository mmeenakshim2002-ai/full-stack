Affiliate Policy

Welcome to the MRM Handcraft Affiliate Program. We believe in the power of community and appreciate our patrons sharing our art with the world. This policy outlines the rules for those who promote our handcrafted products.

Ethical Promotion

As an ambassador for MRM Handcraft, we ask that you represent our brand with integrity.

  • Authenticity: We encourage honest reviews and personal stories about our crafts.
  • Transparency: Per legal requirements, you must clearly disclose to your audience that you receive a commission for any sales made through your link (e.g., using hashtags like #ad or #affiliate).

Commission & Payments

  • Earnings: Affiliates earn a [X]% commission on every successful purchase made through their unique referral link.
  • Cookie Duration: Our tracking cookies last for [e.g., 30 days]. If a patron clicks your link and buys within that window, you get the credit.
  • Payouts: Payments are made via [e.g., PayPal/Bank Transfer] on the [e.g., 1st of every month] once you reach the minimum threshold of [e.g., ₹1,000 / $50].

Prohibited Activities

To protect the MRM Handcraft brand, the following actions are strictly prohibited:

  • Spamming: Sending unsolicited emails or posting links on random forums/comments.
  • Misleading Claims: Making false claims about the materials or shipping times of our products.
  • Coupon Sites: Posting your affiliate link on "coupon" or "discount" database websites.
  • Self-Referrals: Using your own link to purchase items for yourself to get a discount.

Intellectual Property

Affiliates are granted a limited license to use MRM Handcraft photos and logos for promotion. However, you may not alter our logos or use our images in a way that suggests you are the company. You are an independent partner.

Termination

MRM Handcraft reserves the right to terminate an affiliate account at any time if these guidelines are violated. Any unpaid commissions earned through prohibited activities will be forfeited.
