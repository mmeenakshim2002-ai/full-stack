Return and Refund Policy

At MRM Handcraft, we take immense pride in the quality and artistry of our work. Because our items are handcrafted with care, we have established the following policy to ensure a fair experience for all our patrons.

The Nature of Handmade Goods

Please note that every MRM piece is unique. Variations in color, texture, and small "imperfections" are characteristic of the handmade process and are not considered defects. We do not offer refunds based on these natural variations.

Returns

  • Eligible Items: We accept returns only on standard (non-custom) items if they are damaged during transit or if the wrong item was sent.
  • Timeframe: You must notify us within [e.g., 48 hours] of receiving your package.
  • Condition: Items must be returned in their original packaging and in the same condition as received.

    • Non-Returnable Items

    To maintain the exclusivity of our craft, the following cannot be returned:

    • Custom/Bespoke Orders: Any item made specifically to your size, color, or design requirements.
    • Sale Items: Items purchased during a promotional sale or from the "Seconds" gallery.
    • Personalized Items: Anything engraved or marked with names/initials.

    Refund Process

    Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

    • Approved Refunds: If approved, your refund will be processed to your original method of payment within [e.g., 7–10] business days.
    • Shipping Costs: Original shipping charges are non-refundable. The patron is responsible for paying the shipping costs for returning an item.

    Damaged Items

    If your order arrives damaged, please take a photo of the packaging and the item immediately and email it to [Your Email]. We will prioritize a replacement or a full refund for any item broken during delivery.
